Ramen is delicous. Here is how to make it at home without spending hours in the kitchen. This recipe in particular is for tsukemen, a ramen dish served with cold noodles. Add some more filler nonsense....

Here is a list of what you will need:

  • 2 packages of dry ramen noodles
  • 1 pound thin sliced pork belly or shabu shabu
  • 2 cups chinese chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp Sake
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 inch piece ginger root
  • 1 tsp dried chili cut into rings
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 negi or long green onion
  • 1 handful dried shiitake mushroom
  • 1 bunch shimeji mushroom
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar

For toppings:

  • 1 soft boiled egg
  • spring onion leaves
  • Bonito flakes
